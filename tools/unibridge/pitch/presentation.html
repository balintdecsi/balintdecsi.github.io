Desirability

The Problem We Solve

The Burden: Moving to Austria involves a highly bureaucratic, confusing, and stressful process for non-EU students.

Current Solutions:

  • Fragmented information across different official sites.
  • Outdated student forums & word-of-mouth.
  • Expensive relocation agencies (€€€).

Pain Points:

  • Missed visa deadlines (appointments open 3 months ahead).
  • Housing scams.
  • Confusion over insurance and registration (Meldezettel).
Desirability

Market Opportunity & Innovation

Opportunity: Thousands of students from Central Asia (KZ, UZ, KG, TJ, TM) apply to Austrian universities every year. The DACH region remains highly attractive.

Our Unique Innovation:

  • Centralized & Localized: Native language support (RU/EN) tailored for specific origin countries.
  • Actionable Tracking: Interactive checklists and progress bars.
  • End-to-End: Covers both pre-arrival (Visa, Housing) and post-arrival (Insurance, Registration).
Lean Startup Principle: Focus on solving a specific, highly painful problem for a niche beachhead market before scaling.

Competitive Analysis

Competitor Strengths Weaknesses (Our Edge)
University Welcome Centers Free, official information. General advice, lacks tailored step-by-step guides for specific non-EU bureaucratic hurdles.
Relocation Agencies Personalized, handle paperwork. Very expensive, targeted at wealthy expats rather than average students.
Forums / Facebook Groups Peer support, specific cases. Unverified, often outdated, disorganized information.

Market Size: Starting with ~5,000+ new Central Asian students annually in Austria, expanding to all non-EU students (~25,000+ annually).

Product-Market Fit & Prototype

User Flow:

  • Select home country.
  • View tailored requirements.
  • Track progress via step-by-step checklists.

Business Model

Value Proposition: Saving time, money, and stress with a clear roadmap.

Revenue Streams:

  • Freemium (B2C): Free basic checklists. Paid premium templates/translation services.
  • Affiliate/Lead Gen (B2B): Partnerships with housing providers (OeAD, private dorms) and insurance (ÖGK, private).

Target Segments:

  • Primary: Students from Central Asia.
  • Secondary: Universities, housing providers.

Distribution: SEO, TikTok/IG content targeted at high school/bachelor graduates, educational consultants.
