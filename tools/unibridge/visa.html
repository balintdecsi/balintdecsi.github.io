Visa

Student Visa (Aufenthaltsbewilligung Studierende) — everything you need to apply

📄 Documents Required

▾
  • Valid passport (at least 12 months validity)
  • Passport-sized biometric photos (35 x 45 mm)
  • University admission letter (Zulassungsbescheid)
  • Proof of financial means (€ 12,707.44/year or blocked account)
  • Proof of accommodation in Austria
  • Health insurance covering Austria
  • Birth certificate (apostilled & translated)
  • Criminal record certificate (apostilled & translated)
  • Completed application form (signed)

📅 Scheduling a Meeting

▾

You must schedule an appointment at the Austrian Embassy or Consulate in your home country.

  • Visit the embassy website for your country to book online
  • Appointments may have 2–6 week wait times — book early
  • Bring all original documents + one copy of each
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time

Tip: Some embassies allow walk-in submission on specific days — check the local website.

💳 Necessary Payments

▾
  • Visa application fee: ~ € 160
  • Residence permit fee: ~ € 160
  • Translation & apostille costs: ~ € 50–150 per document
  • Blocked account deposit: € 12,707.44 (refundable, drawn monthly)
  • Health insurance: ~ € 60–80 / month

Payments are usually made by bank transfer; some embassies accept card.

⏳ Application Timeline

▾
6–8 months before Receive university admission & start gathering documents
4–6 months before Open blocked bank account, arrange insurance & housing
3–4 months before Book embassy appointment & get translations/apostilles
2–3 months before Submit application at the embassy
6–12 weeks before Wait for visa decision (processing time varies)
2 weeks before Receive visa & book flights
