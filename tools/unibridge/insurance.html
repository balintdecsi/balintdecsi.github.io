Health Insurance

Student health insurance in Austria — providers, costs, and how to sign up

🏢 Insurance Providers

▾
  • ÖGK (Austrian Health Insurance) € 65.73 / mo
  • FeelSafe by Generali € 62 / mo
  • UNIQA StudentProtect € 58 / mo
  • Care Concept (Care College) € 50 / mo

Note: ÖGK is the standard public insurance accepted everywhere. Private options may have limitations.

💰 Insurance Payments

▾

Self-insured students pay a monthly contribution directly to the insurer.

  • ÖGK student rate: € 65.73 / month (2024/25)
  • Payment methods: Direct debit (SEPA), bank transfer, or Erlagschein
  • Payment frequency: Monthly or quarterly
  • Penalty for late payment: Coverage may lapse after 6 weeks

If you work part-time (up to Geringfügigkeitsgrenze of € 518.44/mo), your employer covers insurance.

📄 Documents Required

▾
  • Valid passport or residence permit
  • Confirmation of enrollment (Studienbestätigung / Inskriptionsbestätigung)
  • Meldezettel (proof of address registration)
  • Austrian bank account details (IBAN)
  • Completed application form from the insurer
  • Passport-sized photo (for e-card)

🗓️ Reserve Timeslot

▾

Register with ÖGK in person at a service center or online:

  • Online: ÖGK Self-Insurance Portal
  • In person: ÖGK Wien, Wienerbergstraße 15–19, 1100 Wien
  • Hours: Mon–Fri 07:30–14:30
  • Wait time: Walk-in, typically 15–45 min

Tip: Arrive early in the morning for shorter wait times. Bring all documents listed above.
