📋 Meldezettel (Address Registration)

▾

You must register your Austrian address within 3 days of moving in.

  • Download the Meldezettel form from wien.gv.at
  • Fill in the form and have your landlord sign it
  • Bring it to any Meldeservice (registration office)
  • Required: passport + signed Meldezettel form
  • Free of charge — no appointment needed

🏛️ MA 35 Residence Permit

▾

After registering your address, you need to apply at MA 35 for your student residence permit (Aufenthaltstitel).

  • Documents needed: Passport, Meldezettel, admission letter, insurance, financial proof, photos
  • Application: Submit online or in person
  • Processing time: 4–8 weeks
  • Pick-up: In person at MA 35 with appointment
